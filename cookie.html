Oatmeal Raisin Cookies


Ingredients:

• 1 cup white whole wheat flour

• 1.5 cups oats

• ½ cup coconut sugar

• 1 tsp cinnamon

• ½ tsp baking soda

• ½ tsp kosher salt

• 1/3 cup melted coconut oil

• 1/3 cup apple sauce

• 1 tbsp ground chia seeds

• 3 tbsp water

• 1 tsp vanilla extract

• 1/3 cup chopped walnuts

• ½ cup raisins

Instructions:

1. Pre-heat oven to 350oF. Line a rimmed baking sheet with a silpat mat or parchment paper.

2. In a large bowl combine oats, white whole wheat flour, light brown sugar, cinnamon, baking soda, and kosher salt. Gently toss everything together.

3. Combine ground chia seeds with water in a small bowl and let rest for 5 minutes or until thickened.

4. Add in chia mixture, coconut oil, apple sauce, and vanilla extract into the bowl with the dry ingredients. Mix everything together until you have a thick, cookie dough consistency.

5. Add in the walnuts and raisins, stir until they are evenly distributed through out the batter.

6. Scoop up a tbsp of dough and use your had to form a ball, place on the baking sheet and squish down a bit with your fingers so they look like fat discs. These cookies don’t spread a lot once they cook, so you want to form the into a cookie shape before baking.

7. Bake for 10 minutes or until lightly browned on top and set through. Repeat until you have used all of your dough. Eat and enjoy!
