Portabella Mushroom Burger


Ingredients:

• 2 whole grains rolls

• 1 clove of garlic

• 1 tbsp of extra virgin olive oil

• ¼ cup balsamic vinegar

• 2 tsp of oregano

• 1 tsp garlic powder

• A pinch of salt and pepper

• 2 portabella mushroom caps, wiped down with a wet towel

• ½ red onion, cut into ½ inch rounds 2 oz of goat cheese

• A couple of handfuls of arugula

Instructions:

1. Combine olive oil, balsamic vinegar, oregano, garlic powder, salt and pepper in a medium bowl. Coat mushrooms and onions in the oil mixture and let sit for about 15 minutes.

2. Heat a grill pan over medium high heat and place mushrooms and onions in the pan and grill for about 5 minutes on each side or until the veggies are tender and cooked through.

3. In the meantime toast up the rolls and rub each toasted roll with a garlic clove. The garlic will melt right in to the toast infusing a rich, garlic flavor through out the bread.

4. Layer arugula, onions, mushrooms, peppers and another handful of arugula on the bottom of the roll. Spread the top half of the roll with goat cheese. Pop on the top and enjoy!!
