Sweet Potato Power Bowl


Ingredients:

Sweet Potatoes

• 1 pound purple sweet potatoes

• 1 tbsp olive oil

• ½ tsp salt

• ¼ tsp black pepper

• ½ tsp curry powder

Quinoa

• 1 cup quinoa, rinsed

• 2 cup low sodium veggie broth, or water

Chickpea Salad

• 1-15 ounce can of chickpeas, drained and rinsed

• ¾ cup bell peppers, diced

• ¼ cup red onion, diced

• 1 tbsp lemon juice

• 2 tsp olive oil

Arugula Salad

• 6 ounces arugula

• 2 tsp olive oil

• 2 tsp lemon juice

Egg Organic Free-Range Eggs

• 2 tsp ghee or butter

• 4 organic eggs, happy eggs

Instructions:

1. Pre-heat oven to 425oF. Rinse and dry the sweet potatoes, before cutting into bite sized chucks.

2. Transfer the sweet potatoes to a rimmed baking sheet and season with olive oil, salt, pepper, and curry powder. Gently toss so all the sweet potatoes are coated with the oil and spices. Bake for 20 minutes or until fork tender.

3. Place quinoa in a small pot with the veggie broth (or water). Bring to a boil, reduce to a simmer, pop on a lid and cook for about 15 minutes or until the liquid has absorbed and the quinoa is nice and fluffy.

4. Place chickpeas in a medium sized bowl along with peppers, onion, lemon and oil. Season with salt and pepper and mix well.

5. Season arugula with olive oil, lemon, salt and pepper; gently toss to coat.

6. Divide all four ingredients amongst 4 medium wide bowls.

7. Heat a small non-stick saute pan over low heat. Add in ghee.

8. Add 1-2 Happy Egg Organic Free Range eggs to the pan and cook for 30 seconds and then place a lid over the top. Cook for 2-3 minutes or until the whites are set through and the yolks a bright and runny.

9. Place on top of power bowl and repeat with the remaining eggs. Enjoy!



Notes:

*If you wanted to meal prep this recipe, prepare the sweet potatoes, quinoa and chickpea salad ahead of time and then store in the fridge for up to 5 days. Once ready to assemble, dress your greens and cook up your eggs. Super fast and easy!
