Chocolate Mug Cake


Ingredients:

• 3 tbsp almond flour

• 2 tbsp cacao powder

• 2 tbsp monk fruit

• 1 egg

• 1 tbsp coconut oil

• ½ tsp vanilla extract

• ¼ tsp baking powder

• A pinch of salt

• 1 tbsp chocolate chips, optional

Instructions:

1. Grease an 8-ounce mug with coconut oil, butter, or cooking spray and set aside.

2. Combine all ingredients in a small spouted cup and mix until you have a nice, rich cake batter.

3. Transfer batter into prepared mug.

4. Sprinkle chocolate chips over the top and cook in the microwave for one minute or until set through and springy on top. All microwave will vary a bit.

5. Enjoy as is, or top with whipped cream and fresh berries.
