Lemon Garlic Baked Chicken Breast


Ingredients:

• 1.5 pounds chicken breast, 3-4 breasts

• 2 tsp lemon zest

• 3 tbsp lemon juice

• 2 tbsp extra virgin olive oil

• 1 tsp Dijon mustard

• 2 cloves garlic, crushed

• ½ tsp kosher salt

• ¼ tsp black pepper

• 2 tsp dried Italian seasoning

• ½ cup plain 2% Greek yogurt

• 1 clove crushed garlic

• 1 tbsp extra virgin olive oil

• ¼ cup chopped dill

Instructions:

1. Pre-heat oven to 425 F.

2. Lay the chicken breasts between two pieces of plastic wrap or wax paper and gently pound with a meat mallet. The goal is to create a uniform thickness all around the breast so the chicken can cook evenly. Place chicken in 88 baking dish.

3. In a small bowl or spouted cup, whisk together lemon zest, lemon juice, extra virgin olive oil, mustard, garlic, salt, pepper and Italian seasonings.

4. Pour marinade over the chicken and lest rest on the counter for 20-30 minutes before baking. This gives the flavors a chance to mingle while the chicken comes to room temperature.

5. Pop in the oven for 20-25 minutes or until the internal temperature of the chicken reaches 160F.

6. Allow the chicken to rest and continue cooking on the counter for 5-10 minuted or until the internal temperature has reached 165F. Serve and enjoy!

7. Combine all the ingredients in a small bowl. Stir well and enjoy!

12. Top with fresh tomatoes and cilantro and enjoy!
