Banana Oatmeal Pancake


Ingredients:

• 2 ripe bananas

• 2 eggs

• 1 cup oatmeal

• ½ tsp cinnamon

• 1 tsp baking powder

• A pinch of kosher salt

• ¼ chopped walnuts

• 1 tbsp coconut oil

Instructions:

1. Combine banana, egg whites, oatmeal, cinnamon, baking powder, and salt in the blender and blend until you have a nice thick batter.

2. Stir in walnuts or any other add-ins you like.

3. Heat a large non-stick skillet over a medium heat and lightly coat with coconut oil.

4. Pour pancake batter in the pan to form small-medium sized pancakes. Cook for 2 minutes or until small bubbles begin to form on the top. Flip and cook for another 2 minutes or until golden brown and set through.

5. Serve with your favorite pancake toppings and enjoy!
