Cast Iron Salmon


Ingredients:

• 16 ounces farm raised salmon, cut into 4 ounces filets

• 1 tsp ghee, or coconut or avocado oil

• 1 tsp kosher salt

• ½ tsp black pepper

Instructions:

1. Pre-heat oven to 450oF and place 12-inch cast iron skillet in the oven to heat up.

2. Use a paper towel to pat both sides of salmon dry. If the skin is wet, it won’t get crispy.

3. Season the salmon skin with kosher salt.

4. Carefully, remove hot pan from the oven and melt ghee in the pan.

5. Add salmon, skin side down, to the pan. Season top of salmon with a little more salt and black pepper and place in the oven for about 15 minutes.

6. Once the salmon is opaque and flaky, it’s ready to serve.



Notes:

* I usually just stick a fork in and twist to make sure my salmon is done.

** General rule of thumb is to cook for 4-6 minutes per every 1/2 inch of thickness. So be sure to adjust cook time as needed.
