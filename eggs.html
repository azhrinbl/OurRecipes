Egg Sammies


Ingredients:

• 6 eggs

• 1 tsp olive oil

• ½ onion chopped

• 2 cloves garlic chopped

• 2 bell peppers chopped

• ½ pound fresh baby spinach chopped

• A pinch of salt and pepper

• 6 english muffins

• 6 slices cheddar cheese

Instructions:

1. Preheat the oven to 350oF. Spray the baking dish with cooking spray or coat with butter or coconut oil.

2. Heat olive oil in a large non-stick saut pan, add onion along with pinch of salt. Saut until onions are translucent. Add in peppers and garlic and cook for another 2 minutes.

3. Add in chopped spinach with a bit more salt and pepper, stir and cook until spinach has wilted down. Shut off heat and let veggies cool a bit.

4. In a large bowl, whisk together the eggs and then slowly stir the cooled veggie mixture into the eggs.

5. Pour the egg and vegetable mixture into baking dish and bake for about 30 minutes, or until the center is set and the outer edges are slightly browned. Allow the eggs to cool, the slice into six pieces.

6. Build the sandwiches by adding one piece of the baked eggs and one slice of cheese to each English Muffin. Wrap each sandwich in plastic wrap and enjoy!



Notes:

* You can keep this in the freezer and heat it up when you want to consume it. This food lasts up to 3 months.

** To reheat, you have a couple of options. Remove from freezer the night before and leave in fridge overnight to defrost. Then when ready to eat place on microwave safe plate and heat for a minute or so or until heated through. The other option is to remove from freezer and leave on counter for 20-30 minutes before removing plastic wrap and heating in microwave until warmed through.
